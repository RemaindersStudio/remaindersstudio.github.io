The Studio

About Remainders

Remainders is an independent game studio founded in 2026 by Amy Maskery, a Games Computing student at Northumbria University and a Newcastle local. We create atmospheric, story-driven horror games grounded in real historical events — honouring the people and places that history left behind.

Our work is built on three principles: historical honesty, atmospheric depth, and silent storytelling. We believe the most unsettling horror is the kind that actually happened.

"The scariest stories are the ones nobody could explain."

Every Remainders title begins with research. Real events, real people, real places — reimagined as interactive experiences that sit with the ambiguity of the historical record rather than resolving it.

April 2026 — Entry 001

The House — Day One

This is day one. The bedroom is the current focus. Today I've been working on the visual foundation — establishing the room layout, colour palette, and atmosphere. The walls are sage green, the floor warm wood, and vines creep in from the corner suggesting the house has been left alone for some time.

The rough puzzle structure for the bedroom is planned. A music box and grandfather clock are key objects. Multiple drawers and shelves contain clues. The key to escape is hidden in the music box under the bed.

This is a learning project. The art will improve. The goal for now is to finish something and release it — not to make something perfect. Progress over perfection. Ship it when it's ready.
